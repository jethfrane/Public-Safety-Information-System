(049) 554-9690

Pamana Medical Center was established in 1990 in Calamba City. For decades of hospital operations, PMC boasts of proven quality healthcare track record amongst the residents of the various communities in Calamba City and its adjacent towns, municipalities, and cities.


In its EFFORT to give the community access to affordable & quality healthcare services, PAMANA MEDICAL CENTER's healthcare providers and employees have organized SAVIOUR MEDICAL and DENTAL COOPERATIVE. It was officially registered with the COOPERATIVE DEVELOPMENT AUTHORITY on February 22, 2002. The Cooperative has since 2003 assumed the management and operations of PAMANA MEDICAL CENTER.
