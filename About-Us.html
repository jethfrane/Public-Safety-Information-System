About Us

Public Safety Information System | Calamba

(PSIS), an emergency information system created by a group of hardworking students from the City College of Calamba. Our team, composed of 5 people: Frane, Enriquez, Gatdula, Gongora, and Loquinario, united with the shared objective of developing a comprehensive platform that provides the public with accurate and precise information during emergencies.


PSIS was developed to promote awareness about critical emergency information. Our mission is to keep the public informed and prepared by offering reliable information about various local government departments in the City of Calamba. Our team is dedicated to providing a user-friendly and accessible website for the City of Calamba, and we continuously strive to enhance and expand our platform to serve our users within and beyond the City of Calamba, Laguna. We appreciate your visit to our website and hope that PSIS can supply you with the necessary information to stay safe in emergencies.
